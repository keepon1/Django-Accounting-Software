Simple Sales WebApp

This web application is designed to suite small and large sales Companies to take records. It is developed to be a user friendly webapp. It needs little or no tutorials to use this webApplication because the terms used are Simple and Understandable. Moreover, the terms used are explained at the tutorial section. Hence, anyone can use it with ease.

Track And Retrive Your Records

This webApplication is disigned in a way that data recorded are been stored in a systematic order. The data recordeed over time will be sorted and arranged when retrived or displayed in a way a user wants.This is been done with a specification from the user. Therefore, tracking and retriving records in this webApp is relatively easy and sorted

Statistics And Data Analysis

This webApp intends to communicate to its user statistically by plotting graphs and charts of performances with available data. This graphs and charts will done daily, monthly and yearly. Data Analysis too will be performed on the data to forecast and aid in decision making.
